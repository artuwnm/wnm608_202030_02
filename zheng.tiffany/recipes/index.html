Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (300 g)
  • Ground beef (½ lb)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Onion, sliced (1 whole)
  • Garlic, minced (3 cloves)
  • White mushrooms, sliced (100 g)
  • Chicken stock (1 cup)
  • Water (2 cups)
  • Tomato paste (160 mL)
  • Soy sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Fish sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef: Turn on Sauté function on the Instant Pot, and add olive oil when hot. Sauté ground beef for about 5 minutes, until you see browning, then remove from pot. Set aside.
  2. Sauté vegetables: Add another tbsp olive oil to coat the pot bottom. Add the onion, garlic, salt and black pepper. Cook until onions are soft and fragrant. Throw in mushrooms, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in tomato chicken stock mixture: Combine chicken stock, water, tomato paste, soy sauce and fish sauce. Pour mixture into pot. Add back the ground beef and the spaghetti. Pressure cook for 5 minutes on High Pressure.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Garnish dish with freshly chopped parsley, and enjoy!

Creamy Guacamole

Ingredients

  • Avocados, mashed (3 whole)
  • Lime, juiced (1 whole)
  • Cilantro, chopped (3 tbsp)
  • Cumin (½ tsp)
  • Garlic, minced (2 cloves)
  • Salt (½ tsp)
  • Black pepper (pinch)
  • Cayenne pepper (pinch)

Instructions

  1. Prep all ingredients and combine in a bowl: Using a fork, mash avocadoes together with lime juice and salt. Add in cilantro, cumin, garlic, and cayenne pepper. And that's it!

Tiffany's Tofu

Ingredients

  • Tofu, diced (450 g)
  • Ground pork (½ lb)
  • Vegetable oil (1 tbsp)
  • Ginger, minced (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic, minced (2 cloves)
  • Sugar (1 tsp)
  • Chinese cooking wine (2 tsp)
  • Spicy mapo tofu sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Spicy fermented bean paste (1 tbsp)
  • Oyster sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Cornstarch (1 tsp)
  • Green onion, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prep ingredients: Cut tofu into cubes. Mince ginger and garlic. Cut green onion and separate the white parts from the green ends. Blanch the tofu in boiling water for a couple of minutes, drain, and set aside.
  2. Sauté aromatics: Heat wok on medium-high heat. Coat wok with vegetable oil. Add the chopped white ends from the green onion, ginger, and garlic. Cook until fragrant, then add ground pork. Sprinkle sugar on the pork.
  3. Cook ground pork: When ground pork starts browning, drizzle in the cooking wine and 1 tbsp of water. Let the pork sizzle in the liquid for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add tofu to wok: Gently mix tofu together with the ground pork. Careful not to break the tofu!
  5. Season tofu and thicken sauce: Add the spicy mapo tofu sauce, spicy fermented bean paste, and oyster sauce. Mix corn starch with water and pour into wok. Let the sauce thicken, then garnish with green onion on top. Serve and enjoy!
