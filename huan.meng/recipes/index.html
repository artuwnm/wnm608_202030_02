Recipe 1

Stew the Leg Meat of Young Chicken with Mushrooms and Chinese Chestnuts

This is one of my favorite dish. It is a perfect match with a bowl of rice, and the main ingredients of the dish matching together can diliver a perfect flavor.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking wine
  • Some of the leg meat of young chicken
  • Some shredded ginger and garlic
  • Mushroom
  • Chinese chestnut which we can eat them just the way they are

Steps

  1. Get rid of the bone of the leg meat of young chicken and cut the meat into small pieces
  2. Add some of the cooking wine and black peppers into the meat
  3. Mix the meat and the seasoning well and put them into the refrigerator for 2 hours
  4. Place the shredded ginger and garlic in to the pan with a little bit oils
  5. Fry the ginger and garlic till smelling a tempting flavor
  6. Add the mushrooms to the pan and fry it
  7. Place the meats into the pan and add some soy sauce, sugar, then add some water
  8. Place the degree of the heating into high, stew for a few minutes till the soup were absorbed into the food
  9. Enjoy it

Stew potatoes with kidney beans

Recipe 3

Spicy pork noodles

It is a dish cooking very quickly and easily.What is more, it is a dish with richable elements that are good for our body.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Pork
  • Shredded Green Pepper
  • Fresh Tasted Soy Sauce
  • Chinese Five Spice Powder
  • Shredded Scallion

Steps

  1. Place the shredded scallions and shredded pork into hot pot
  2. Add Chinese spice powder and soy sauce fry till smelling a kind of good scent
  3. Add the green pepper into the pan and add a little bit salt till the green pepper getting tender
  4. Add some cold water into the pot
  5. Cook until boil and Remove from heat
  6. Cook noodles until boil
  7. Flush noodles with Cool water
  8. Place the Noodles into the plate
  9. Watering the soup on the noodles
  10. Enjoy it
