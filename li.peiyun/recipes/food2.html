Spicy Chicken Cold Noodle

  1. Put sticky rice in water for over 30 minutes, and then throw out the water.
  2. Mix 500g ground meat with 1 raw egg and some chopped carrots.
  3. Hold the prepared meat into ball shape, and roll on the sticky rice to get covered.
  4. Place the meat balls in plate and steam for about 30 minutes.
  5. Put on some decoration and enjoy!
