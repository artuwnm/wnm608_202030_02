French Onion Soup

❮ ❯

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Onions
  • 1 bowl of beef stock
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Rosemary
  • sliced French bread
  • 1 cup of grated Swiss cheese

Steps:

  1. Cut the onions into thin slices.
  2. Heat the pot with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and toss the sliced onions into the pot.
  3. Cook and stir the onions constantly until they have softened, around 15 minutes.
  4. Turn up the heat, add sugar and salt to the onions and continue to cook until they turned golden brown.
  5. Add the flour to the pot and mix well with the onions.
  6. Add red wine to the pot and scrape up the browned bits on the bottom of the pot. Feel free to add more wine if you like.
  7. Add the beef stock (just slightly over the onions) and the rosemary. Bring them to a simmer.
  8. Cover the pot and reduce the heat to maintain a low simmer for around 30 minutes.
  9. Toast the sliced French bread to golden brown while the soup is simmering.
  10. After 30 minutes, season the soup with more salt and pepper if needed. Discard the rosemary.
  11. Ladle soup into an oven-proof bowl and put the toasted bread on top of it.
  12. Sprinkle generous amount of cheese on top of the bread.
  13. Put the bowl of soup into the oven and broil it until the cheese turned golden brown. Enjoy!

Carbonara with Mushroom

❮ ❯

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound pasta (I used fettuccine this time)
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 mushroom
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of whipping cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and add the pasta in. Cook till al dente.
  2. Cut the bacon and mushroom into bite-size.
  3. Add the bacon into a pan and cook until it turns crispy.
  4. Add the sliced mushroom into the pan after the bacon has turned crispy.
  5. After the bacon and mushroom are cooked, turn off the heat and wait until the pasta is done.
  6. In a small bowl, beat the egg with whipping cream, salt and pepper. Add half of the cheese as well.
  7. When the pasta is done, transfer it to the pan with bacon and mushroom. Mix it up a little bit.
  8. Add the creamy sauce into the pan and stir it up quickly with the pasta. (Remember to turn off the heat or you will get scramble eggs!)
  9. After it is well-mixed, plate the pasta and sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top. Enjoy!

Easy Egg & Avocado Tortilla

❮ ❯

Ingredients

  • Tortilla (I used roti this time but it tastes the same)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 avocado
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the tortilla till it is hot.
  2. Remove the tortilla from the pan, reduce the heat and add 1 teaspoon of oil.
  3. Add an egg to the pan and quickly put the tortilla on the top of the egg. Gently press down so that the egg yolk breaks.
  4. Filp the tortilla when the egg is sticked to the tortilla.
  5. Add cheese and avovado on top of the egg.
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top.
  7. Gently fold the tortilla and remove it from heat until cheese is melted. Enjoy!
