1.Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Small Avocado
  • 1 Slice of Weat Bread
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Tablespoon of Lemon Juice
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Bacon

How to Make It

  1. In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Gently mash with the back of a fork.
  3. Bake a bacon as much as you want.
  4. When the water boils, put an egg and wait for 8mins.
  5. Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle over desired toppings.

2.Alio-e-Olio

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Spaghetti Noodle
  • 3 Table Spoon of Minced Garlic
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Red Pepper
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup of Extra-Virgin Olive Oil

How to Make It

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water about 12 minutes.
  2. Drain and transfer to a pasta bowl.
  3. Combine garlic and olive oil in a cold skillet.
  4. Cook over medium heat to slowly toast garlic, about 10 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low when olive oil begins to bubble.
  5. Cook and stir until garlic is golden brown, about another 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  6. Serve pasta topped with the remaining Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.

3.Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of New York Steak
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 Tablespoon of Extra-Virgin Olive Oil

How to Make It

  1. Bring a pan and start heating on medium fire.
  2. Put olive oil.
  3. When the pan is heated, put the meat in.
  4. Cook over medium heat to slowly toast the surface of the meat, about 5 minutes.
  5. Flip to the other side.
  6. Place on a plate with salad or sidedishes and eat.
