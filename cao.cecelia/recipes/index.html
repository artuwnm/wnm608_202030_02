  5. The appropriate amount of mushroom

  7. Tallow (* can replace salad oil)
  8. Soy sauce 60 ml
  9. Mirin 50 ml
  10. Wine 50 ml
  11. Cook 1 spoon
  12. 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  13. Water 150 ml
  14. One raw egg for each
  • Directions
  1. Cut the material first
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Put all the seasonings in the pan and let them melt overheat (don't let them boil)
  4. Prepare the pot, fire the oil

  6. Add the beef slices 1/3 Fried
  7. Add all the ingredients and sauce and cook until done
  8. Add the remaining slices of beef at the end

Dongpo Pork

  • Ingredients
  1. Pork belly 800 grams
  2. star anise 15 grams
  3. piece of bay leaf
  4. 50 grams of green onion
  5. 30 grams of ginger
  6. 20 grams of sugar
  7. salt
  8. soy sauce 25 grams
  9. 30 grams of rice wine
  10. 25 grams of rock sugar
  11. 5 grams of peanut oil
  • Directions
  1. first cut the pork belly into 2.5cm square pieces, with cold water under the pot, boil the meat pieces after 5 minutes to fish out, control water reserve
  2. Wok heat into a little oil, stir-fry star anise, pour in fine sugar stir-fry sugar color until slightly yellow can
  3. Sugar thoroughly melt, fry to a little color, under the meat pieces stir-fry, the meat pieces stir-fry to dry water color transparent, yellow surface, and began to oil cooking rice wine
  4. Add soy sauce stir fry, soy sauce evenly adsorbed on the meat
  5. The meat Fried, into the pot into the boiling water, into the spring onion, ginger, geranium
  6. Add rock sugar, cover the pot and simmer for 30-40 minutes
  7. After the meat stewed, pick up the onion, ginger, star anise, geranium do not, then, put a little salt, with a high heat juice

Oil Spill Noodle

  • Ingredients
  1. flour 100 grams
  2. warm soy milk
  3. 7-8 leek
  4. 3 spinach
  5. pork belly a small piece
  6. 1/4 onion
  7. radish pickles 10 grams
  8. half green onions
  9. garlic 8 cloves
  10. soy sauce 1 tablespoon
  11. vinegar 1 tablespoon
  12. sesame oil a few drops
  13. salt 1 tablespoon
  • Directions
  1. warm soybean milk and noodles, also can use lukewarm water, the morning dozen soybean milk drink not to drop just used the soft and hard moderate good, after kneading smooth set aside for 20 minutes
  2. In the process of processing other materials, pork belly cut, onion cut into small pieces, pan into the oil, onion chopped saute, add pork belly stir fry
  3. Add a little soy sauce and radish and pickles until the meat changes color. Continue to stir-fry for 1 minute
  4. Roll the dough into cakes. The thickness of the cakes can be changed according to your taste
  5. After the noodles are cooked, use cold water. Keep the water dry and place it in the noodle bowl
  6. Blanch the spinach and leek and remove the dried water and place on top of the noodles.
  7. pot into the oil, the amount of the same as usual, oil hot after the spill on the surface, delicious oil spill on the surface is OK
