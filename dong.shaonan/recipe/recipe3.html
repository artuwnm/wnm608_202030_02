PAPAYA SALAD

WHAT YOU PREPARE
  • 1 Papaya
  • Chopped red chili
  • Ground peanut
  • 1 Passion fruit
  • Half lime
  • 1/2 salt
  • 3 tea spoon Fish oil
    • INSTRUCTIONS
  • 1. Slice the papaya
  • 2. Store the slices in cold water
  • 3. Add chili, peanut, passion fruit juice, lime juice and salt
  • 4. Add salt and fish oil at last
